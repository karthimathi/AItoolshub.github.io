Introduction to Consensus AI

Consensus AI is an advanced artificial intelligence platform specifically designed for research analysis and evidence synthesis. It serves as an intelligent research assistant that helps academics, students, professionals, and curious minds navigate the vast landscape of scientific literature to find trustworthy answers based on actual research evidence.

Consensus AI democratizes access to scientific knowledge by using AI to read, understand, and synthesize findings from millions of research papers, making evidence-based insights accessible to everyone.

Unlike general AI chatbots, Consensus AI is trained specifically on peer-reviewed academic literature and uses proprietary algorithms to identify consensus positions, methodological quality, and evidence strength across scientific studies. The platform provides citations and confidence levels for every answer, maintaining academic integrity and transparency.

From medical professionals seeking treatment evidence to students writing research papers, Consensus AI bridges the gap between complex academic research and practical, actionable insights‚Äîall while maintaining the rigor and credibility of traditional academic research methods.

How to Use Consensus AI

Basic Research Queries

Start by asking research questions in natural language. Consensus AI understands questions about specific topics, relationships between concepts, treatment effectiveness, intervention outcomes, and methodological questions.

Question Formatting Best Practices

  • Ask specific, research-oriented questions
  • Use academic terminology when appropriate
  • Ask about relationships: "Does X affect Y?"
  • Request evidence summaries: "What does research say about Z?"
  • Ask for methodological guidance: "What are best practices for measuring A?"

Advanced Research Features

  • Filter results by study type, sample size, and publication date
  • Request systematic review-style summaries
  • Compare conflicting study findings
  • Ask about research gaps and future directions
  • Request citation export in various academic formats

Evidence Evaluation Tools

Consensus AI provides confidence scores, study quality indicators, consensus strength metrics, and identifies areas of research agreement vs. controversy. Use these tools to critically evaluate the evidence presented.
